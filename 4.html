BLOOD DONATION

I worked in the French Blood Establishment from October 2019 to October 2021. During this period, I had the opportunity to work as the graphic designer of the Communication Coordination team co-creating visuals for social networks (videos, gifs, stickers, images, Instagram challenges…), email visuals, and also some prints assets (infographics, posters…). Most of this time, I created pedagogical visuals to promote blood donation, and I also participated in the creation of some content for the internal affairs. All this content was created respecting the brand values and the already defined graphic chart.

The utilisation of blood products

This is a pedagogical video about the use of the blood products, which was made to be diffused in the TV screens around the collection site, and were also adapted to be shared through social networks.

SOME EXEMPLES OF CONTENT FOR social networks

Some examples of digital and print visuals I created for the "Prenez le relais 2021" initiative, which invites people to donate blood, and also to share the message to other people to create a chain of solidarity.

MISSING TYPE

Missing type is a global campaign born in the UK and adapted in many countries worldwide. This campaign consists of inviting organizations to remove the letters “A”, “O”, and “B” from their signatures and social media branding to bring attention to the ‘missing types’ of donated blood to encourage new blood donors. Recently, the initiative has also invited influencers and people in general, to temporally remove these letters from their Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter profiles.

These are some examples of content we created for the “Missing Type” campaign. The idea of these visuals was to play with the concept of erasing the letters “A”, “O”, and “B”, tagging dierent food and drink companies, to motivate them to join the campaign.
