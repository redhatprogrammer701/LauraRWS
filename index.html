ABOUT

LAURA RODRIGUEZ

I’m a Graphic and Motion Designer. I was born and raised in Medellin, Colombia but I have lived abroad for the last 5 years. I first studied Product Design Engineering at EAFIT University (Colombia), where I developed an analytic dimension of projects and learned a lot of research tools. Then I have studied in the superior art school of Marseille (France) and “l’école multimedia” in Paris, which has given me an artistic and practical vision of multimedia projects.

EXPERIENCE

Visual arts are my biggest passion in life and I do enjoy all the steps involved in the creative process. I have made projects for French and Colombian enterprises (I’m also ready to add more countries to this list). Some companies I’ve had the pleasure to work with are “l'Etablissement français du sang”, “Synchronne”, “Le Rooftop de Prado”, “PROPLAS S.A.S”, “BOTANIKA” ... I can’t wait to work together!!
