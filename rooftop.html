ROOFTOP DE PRADO

I worked for the bar-restaurant “Le Rooftop de Prado” from Jun 2019 until October 2019 in Marseille, France. I developed the visual identity for the visit cards, menu, and the typographie, the logo was initially provided by the client.



THE MENU

THE VISIT CARDS

For the Visit Cards the idea was to keep the initial referent of handmade style.

the small Latino neighborhood

I also participated in the decoration of the place, painting the walls of the stairs leading to the rooftop, and painting some decoration details for the place. My client wanted to recreate a Latino ambiance, so we did a small Latino neighborhood with typical windows and doors. For the painting part, I worked with my friend July Meng.
